# Installation Guide

Follow these steps to add the provided Volume Profile indicators to your TradingView charts:

1.  **Open TradingView:**
    Log in to your TradingView account and open a chart.

2.  **Open the Pine Editor:**
    At the bottom of the TradingView interface, click on the "Pine Editor" tab.

3.  **Create a New Indicator:**
    Click on "Open" > "New blank indicator".

4.  **Copy and Paste the Code:**
    *   Open either `volume_profile.pine` or `volume_profile_rows.pine` from this repository.
    *   Copy the entire contents of the file.
    *   Paste the code into the Pine Editor in TradingView, replacing any default code that's already there.

5.  **Save the Script:**
    Click the "Save" button in the Pine Editor, give the script a name (e.g., "PD RTH VP"), and click "Save".

6.  **Add to Chart:**
    Click "Add to Chart" in the Pine Editor toolbar to apply the indicator to your current active chart.
