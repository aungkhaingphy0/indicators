# Installation Guide

Follow these steps to add any of the Volume Profile indicators to your TradingView charts:

1.  **Open TradingView**: Log in and open a chart.
2.  **Open Pine Editor**: Click the "Pine Editor" tab at the bottom.
3.  **Create New Script**: Click "Open" > "New blank indicator".
4.  **Paste Code**:
    *   Choose a script from this repository (Recommended: `volume_profile_combined.pine`).
    *   Copy the 100% of the code.
    *   Paste it into the editor, replacing all existing text.
5.  **Save**: Click "Save", name it (e.g., "RTH VP Combined"), and confirm.
6.  **Add to Chart**: Click "Add to Chart" to see the levels.
